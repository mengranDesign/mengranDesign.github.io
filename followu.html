FOLLOW U

Auto-following shopping cart

Follow U is an auto-following shopping cart that is designed for old people to help them carrying heavy goods.

Sep.2013 - Jun.2014

Beijing, China


My role:

User research

Idea proposition

Prototype

Context

The aging process has become the trend of the society. In 2010, there are around 8 percent of the world’s population are aged 65 or older.

By 2050, this number is expected to nearly triple to about 1.5 billion, representing 16 percent of the world’s population. The old people is a group that society can’t ignore.

User Research

For old people, shopping is one of the most important and frequent activities in their daily lives. However people' physical conditions have declined as they grow older. They walk slowly and can’t carry heavy things.

Solution

An auto-following shopping car could be the solution.

Prototype

Production

With limited time, we developed the cart base can carry maximum 10kg goods and follow people automatically with 1m to 1.5m distance.
