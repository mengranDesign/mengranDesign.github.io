FOLLOW U

Auto-following shopping cart for old people

Duration

Sep.2013 - Jun.2014

My role

Idea proposition

User Research

Prototype

Method

User Centered Design

Deliverable

Shopping Cart Prototype

Context

The aging process has become the trend of the society. In 2010, there are around 8 percent of the world’s population are aged 65 or older.



By 2050, this number is expected to nearly triple to about 1.5 billion, representing 16 percent of the world’s population. The old people is a group that society can’t ignore.

User Research

For old people, shopping is one of the most important and frequent activities in their daily lives. However, as people grow old, their physical conditions have declined a lot. They have experienced many difficulties in the shopping activities. They walk slowly, may need walking sticks and can’t carry heavy things.

To understand what they really need when they go out for shoppings, we did some studies on their shopping habits through interview and observations.

Solution

Prototype

1st Version

With limited time, we produced our first version of the product. The first version has the auto-following base and can carry maximum 10kg goods. It could follow people and maintain around 1-1.5m distance.
