Stop&Go

A personal raid transit transportation system

Stop&Go is a concept design for Amsterdam in 2040. Considering the rapid population growth and the use of new technology, the city will be more crowded in 20 years. A new system for rapid transit between personal use and public transportation is proposed in this project.

Concept design

Amsterdam in 2040

Our design is based on the scenario: on the one hand, more and more people become free lancers and tend to work more flesibly and they have the strong need to travel individually. On the other hand, the condensed Amsterdam has limited capacity for growing personal vehicles. The conflict between personal requirement and public capacity is the issue that we focused.

Scenario

Design

We aim to offer an alternative towards the massive transport systems but with the advantage of being able to join a quicker option of transportation when speed is a crucial factor.

The concept has three main components:

  1. Personal pod: Ugo
  2. Transporter bus: Wego
  3. Station
